Privacy Policy
Effective Date: February 19, 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to TrendBeacon. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application to integrate with your TikTok account for analytics, trending data research, and metric aggregation.

2. Information We Collect
When you authorize TrendBeacon through the TikTok integration, we request access to:

Profile Information: Such as your username and display name.

Video Analytics: Data regarding your video performance (views, likes, comments, shares, watch time).

Public Content Data: Information regarding trending sounds and hashtags.
We do not collect passwords or secure credentials. Authentication is handled directly and securely by TikTok.

3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data solely to:

Provide you with a consolidated dashboard of your account performance.

Generate historical engagement reports and analytics.

Surface market signals (trending sounds and hashtags) for content research.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell, rent, or share your TikTok data with any third parties, advertisers, or external data brokers. Your data is used strictly within TrendBeacon to provide the requested analytics services.

5. Data Retention and Revoking Access
Data retrieved from TikTok is stored only as long as necessary to provide the analytics service. You may revoke TrendBeacon's access to your TikTok account at any time by navigating to your TikTok app settings (Settings and privacy > Security > Manage app permissions) and removing the integration. Upon revocation, we will no longer access your data.
